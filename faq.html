Frequently Asked Questions

What is Quizzy?

Quizzy is an interactive platform that offers engaging quizzes on various topics to make learning fun.

How do I create a quiz?

You can create a quiz by signing up, navigating to the "Create Quiz" section, and following the easy steps.

Is Quizzy free to use?

Yes! Quizzy offers a free version with basic features. There are also premium options for additional features.

Can I track my progress?

Yes, Quizzy allows you to track your quiz performance, scores, and improvements over time.

How do I contact support?

You can reach out to our support team via email at support@quizzy.com.
